# Phase 23: Guest Success Page - Context

**Gathered:** 2026-03-14
**Status:** Ready for planning

<domain>
## Phase Boundary

After OTP verification, guest users see a dedicated success page at `/launch/success/guest` with welcome-to-community messaging, email safelist reminder, and social media links with custom icons. Replaces the existing stub page. Uses the PostSignupLayout and design language established in Phase 22.

</domain>

<decisions>
## Implementation Decisions

### Welcome messaging tone
- Community belonging: "You're one of us" — emphasizes community over transaction
- Reuse green check circle with pulse animation from verify page (consistent across flow)
- Copy density: Claude's discretion — copy-writer skill determines right length for tone
- Blend table metaphors in headline with community language in body — bridge from signup to belonging
- Copy generated by tenseats-copy-writer skill during planning, reviewed at checkpoint

### Page content structure
- Section order: Headline → Green check → VerticalTimeline (next steps) → Social links section
- No CTA button at bottom — page is a destination, social links are the only actions
- Auth guard: only users who have completed OTP verification can access — others redirect to `/launch`

### Email safelist presentation
- Inline as one step in the VerticalTimeline next-steps — not a standalone card, not a footnote
- Click-to-copy button next to the `supportteams@tenseats.io` email address
- Timeline steps: 1) Add supportteams@tenseats.io to contacts (with copy button) 2) Follow us on social 3) Explore soon — teaser step with no link, signals more is coming

### Social links layout
- Standalone "Connect with us" section below the VerticalTimeline — separate section with visual weight
- Larger icons with text labels (not just icon row)
- Open in new tabs (target="_blank")
- Instagram URL: `https://www.instagram.com/tenseats`
- Pinterest URL: `https://pinterest.com/tenseats` (placeholder — update when real URL exists)
- Uses InstagramIcon and PinterestIcon from `components/icons/social-icons.tsx` (Phase 21)

### Claude's Discretion
- Copy length and exact wording (copy-writer skill generates)
- Icon sizes in the social section
- Spacing between sections
- How the "explore soon" teaser step reads
- Click-to-copy UX details (toast notification, icon change)

</decisions>

<specifics>
## Specific Ideas

- "You're one of us" community feeling — not a transactional confirmation
- Table metaphor in headline, community in body — "Your seat at the table is set" then community language
- VerticalTimeline reused from Phase 22 with guest-specific steps
- Click-to-copy for the email address — reduces friction
- "Explore soon" step is a teaser — no link yet, signals the platform is coming
- Social section is its own moment below the timeline, not crammed into it

</specifics>
